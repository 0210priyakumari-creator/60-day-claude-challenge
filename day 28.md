# 🏥 Day 28 – Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator

Today's project focuses on simulating one of the most important administrative workflows in healthcare—Hospital Admission Readiness.

This interactive simulator allows users to step into the role of a **Hospital Admission Coordinator** and experience the decision-making process behind preparing a patient for admission.

## 🚀 Features

* Patient admission setup with diagnosis and admission type
* Prior Authorization (PA) workflow

  * Approved
  * Pending
  * Denied → Appeal Process
* Admission Readiness Score
* Weighted scoring model
* CMS Observation Status (2-Midnight Rule)
* InterQual & Milliman medical necessity reminders
* Insurance verification
* Bed assignment workflow
* Documentation & Physician Orders tracking
* Consent completion
* Care Coordination dashboard
* Utilization Review responsibilities
* Risk tracking across multiple categories
* Timeline-based admission workflow
* Governance Snapshot with healthcare industry benchmarks
* Final Admit / Not Ready decision engine

## 💡 What I Learned

* Healthcare admissions involve far more than assigning a hospital bed.
* Prior Authorization significantly impacts patient flow.
* Clinical documentation directly affects reimbursement and denial risk.
* Utilization Review plays a critical role in ensuring medical necessity.
* Care coordination requires collaboration across multiple hospital teams.
* Administrative readiness can be modeled using weighted scoring systems.
