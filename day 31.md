# 🚀 AI Supply Chain Control Tower

A premium, browser-based operations simulation where you step into the role of **Head of Operations** and manage a global supply chain under constant disruption.

This project is designed to demonstrate how operational decisions influence business performance through an engaging, real-time simulation. Every alert requires strategic thinking, and every decision impacts key business metrics.

## ✨ Features

* 📊 Live KPI Dashboard

  * Service Level
  * Customer Satisfaction
  * Inventory Health
  * Transportation Efficiency
  * Operating Cost
  * Revenue Protected
  * Score
  * Countdown Timer

* 🚨 Dynamic Operational Alerts

  * Port Congestion
  * Supplier Delays
  * Truck Breakdowns
  * Warehouse Stockouts
  * Customs Inspections
  * Demand Spikes
  * Factory Failures
  * Weather Disruptions
  * Inventory Mismatches
  * Damaged Shipments

* 🎯 Decision-Based Gameplay

  * Expedite Shipments
  * Use Backup Suppliers
  * Transfer Inventory
  * Increase Production
  * Approve Air Freight
  * Reroute Transportation
  * Delay Decisions
  * Ignore Alerts

* 📈 Real-Time Business Impact

  * Every decision changes operational KPIs.
  * Incorrect decisions increase costs and reduce customer satisfaction.
  * Some actions create delayed consequences, simulating real-world supply chain complexity.

* 🎮 Interactive Experience

  * Premium dark Operations Control Center UI
  * Animated KPI cards
  * Live event log
  * Escalating difficulty
  * Multiple simultaneous disruptions
  * Performance grading system
  * Pause functionality
  * Help modal
  * Responsive design

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* HTML5
* CSS3
* Vanilla JavaScript

No frameworks, no external libraries, and no backend—everything runs completely offline in a single HTML file.

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

This project helps users understand:

* Supply Chain Operations
* Logistics Management
* Decision Making Under Pressure
* Risk Management
* Inventory Optimization
* Transportation Planning
* Business KPI Analysis
* Operational Leadership

## 💡 Why I Built This

Modern supply chains are constantly challenged by disruptions. This simulator transforms those challenges into an interactive learning experience, allowing users to explore operational trade-offs and understand how strategic decisions affect overall business performance.
