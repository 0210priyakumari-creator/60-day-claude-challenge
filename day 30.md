# 🚚 Supply Chain Builder

**Supply Chain Builder** is a single-file React application designed to help beginners understand how modern supply chains work through interactive decision-making.

Instead of reading long theory, users build a complete supply chain by making real business decisions—choosing suppliers, factory locations, warehouse strategies, transportation methods, and inventory policies. Every choice includes a simple explanation of what it means, why it matters, and the trade-offs involved.

### ✨ Features

* Random company generated every playthrough
* Step-by-step guided learning experience
* Real-time business metrics

  * Cost
  * Delivery Speed
  * Risk
  * Customer Satisfaction
  * Sustainability
* Enterprise-style analytics dashboard
* Overall Supply Chain Score (0–100)
* Personalized strengths, weaknesses, biggest risk, and improvement recommendations
* Responsive premium dark UI
* Built with React (CDN), JSX, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
* Runs completely offline in a single HTML file

### 🎯 Learning Outcome

This project demonstrates how strategic decisions across sourcing, manufacturing, warehousing, transportation, and inventory influence business performance. It is designed to make complex supply chain concepts approachable for students, beginners, and anyone curious about operations management.

Every decision tells a story—and every story shapes the supply chain.
