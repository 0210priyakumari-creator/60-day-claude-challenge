# 🚀 Startup Validation Report Dashboard

This project presents a professional startup validation report designed in a VC-style consulting format. The report analyzes **SkillBridge AI**, an AI-powered career readiness and job placement platform for students and fresh graduates.

## 📊 Key Insights

* Market Opportunity Analysis (TAM, SAM, SOM)
* Founder-Market Fit Evaluation
* Competitor Benchmarking
* Customer Persona & ICP Research
* Risk Assessment Framework
* Revenue Model & Financial Projections
* Go / No-Go Investment Recommendation
* 30-Day Startup Execution Plan

## 🎯 Objective

To demonstrate how startup ideas can be evaluated using structured frameworks commonly used by venture capital firms, accelerators, and consulting organizations.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills

* Market Research
* Startup Strategy
* Business Analysis
* Data Visualization
* Product Thinking
* Entrepreneurial Finance

## 📈 Outcome

The analysis concludes that SkillBridge AI addresses a significant market gap in the education-to-employment ecosystem, with strong scalability and revenue potential, making it a promising startup opportunity.
